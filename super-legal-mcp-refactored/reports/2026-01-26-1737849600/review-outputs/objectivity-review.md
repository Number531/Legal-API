### Objectivity Best Practices Observed

**1. Conditional Language Pattern:**
- "may result in," "risk of," "if X then Y," "potential liability"
- NOT: "will cause," "certain to," "guaranteed outcome"

**2. Counter-Argument Integration:**
- Present finding → acknowledge counter-argument → explain why finding still holds OR residual risk remains
- NOT: Present finding → ignore counter-argument

**3. Uncertainty Flagging:**
- "Unknown whether...", "Requires X diligence to determine...", "Settlement outcome depends on..."
- NOT: Speculative assumptions presented as facts

**4. Probability Distribution:**
- Base case / Downside / Severe scenarios with explicit percentages
- NOT: Single-point estimates or binary outcomes

**5. Citation Support:**
- Legal conclusions tied to cited statute/case/regulation
- NOT: Unsupported assertions or "as everyone knows" claims
