### A. TWIC Reader Requirements Under 33 CFR Â§ 105.255

#### 1. Electronic TWIC Inspection vs. Visual Inspection

**Statutory Authority**: 46 U.S.C. Â§ 70105 (Maritime Transportation Security Act of 2002, as amended); 33 CFR Part 105 Subpart B (Facility Security Requirements).

**Access Control Requirements â€” 33 CFR Â§ 105.255(a):**

The facility owner or operator must ensure that access to the facility is controlled, and that access points and areas within the facility are controlled in accordance with the FSP (Facility Security Plan). 33 CFR Â§ 105.255(a).

**TWIC Inspection Requirement â€” 33 CFR Â§ 105.255(b):**

Facilities must implement a TWIC program "as set out in subpart E of part 101 of this subchapter, as applicable, and in accordance with the facility's assigned Risk Group." 33 CFR Â§ 105.255(b).

**Risk Group A Electronic Inspection Mandate:**

Under the 2016 TWIC Reader Final Rule (81 Fed. Reg. 57651), the Coast Guard eliminated the three-tier risk group structure and created a bifurcated system:

- **Risk Group A Facilities**: Must conduct **electronic TWIC inspection** for all individuals seeking unescorted access to secure areas. Electronic inspection verifies credential authenticity through biometric matching (fingerprint) and checks TSA's Canceled Card List. 33 CFR Â§Â§ 105.253, 105.255(c).

- **Non-Risk Group A Facilities**: May use **visual TWIC inspection** (examining the card visually for tampering, verifying photo matches individual, checking expiration date). 33 CFR Â§ 105.255(b).

**Risk Group A Facility Definition â€” 33 CFR Â§ 105.253(a):**

A facility is designated Risk Group A if it meets ANY of the following criteria:

1. **CDC Bulk Handling**: Handles Certain Dangerous Cargo (CDC) in bulk as defined in 33 CFR Â§ 101.105 (includes LNG, LPG, ammonia, chlorine, sulfur dioxide, and other hazardous materials listed in 46 CFR 150.05).

2. **CDC Vessel Reception**: Receives vessels carrying CDC in bulk but does not transfer CDC during the vessel-facility interface.

3. **Cruise Ship Terminals**: Receives vessels certificated to carry more than 1,000 passengers.
