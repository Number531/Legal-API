### A. Legal Framework: IMO Carbon Intensity Indicator (CII)

#### 1. MARPOL Annex VI Amendments - IMO MEPC.328(76) [VERIFIED]

On June 17, 2021, the International Maritime Organization's Marine Environment Protection Committee (MEPC 76) adopted resolution MEPC.328(76), introducing amendments to MARPOL Annex VI containing short-term greenhouse gas reduction measures.¬π These amendments established two critical regulatory mechanisms:

- **Energy Efficiency Existing Ship Index (EEXI)**: Technical measure requiring one-time compliance verification
- **Carbon Intensity Indicator (CII)**: Operational measure requiring annual compliance rating

The amendments entered into force on **November 1, 2022**, with requirements for EEXI and CII certification taking effect from **January 1, 2023**.¬≤

#### 2. CII Rating System (A-E Scale)

Starting January 1, 2023, all ships of 5,000 gross tonnage and above must calculate and report their operational Carbon Intensity Indicator (CII), which links CO‚ÇÇ emissions to the ship's capacity over distance traveled.¬≥ The rating system classifies vessels from A (best) to E (worst):

| Rating | Performance Category | Distribution | Status |
|--------|---------------------|--------------|--------|
| **A** | Major Superior | Top 20% | Exceeds requirements |
| **B** | Minor Superior | Next 20% | Above requirements |
| **C** | Moderate | Middle 30% | **COMPLIANT** |
| **D** | Minor Inferior | Next 20% | Below requirements |
| **E** | Inferior | Bottom 10% | Significantly below requirements |

**CII Calculation Formula:**
```
CII = (Mass of CO‚ÇÇ emitted) / (Transport work)
Where: Transport work = Cargo capacity (DWT) √ó Distance traveled (nautical miles)
Units: gCO‚ÇÇ/ton-mile
```

#### 3. Three-Consecutive-Year Rule - Mandatory Corrective Action [CRITICAL]

**IMO MEPC.337(76) Section 2.1.3**: A ship rated **D for three consecutive years**, or **E for one year**, must submit a corrective action plan demonstrating how the required index of **C or above** will be achieved.‚Å¥

**SEEMP Part III Requirements:**
- The Ship Energy Efficiency Management Plan (SEEMP) Part III must be updated with a corrective action plan
- The revised SEEMP must be submitted within **one month** after reporting the attained annual operational CII
- The corrective action plan must be **verified by the flag state Administration or a Recognized Organization (RO)** before a Statement of Compliance can be issued‚Åµ
- Ships cannot receive their Statement of Compliance without completing this process‚Å∂

**Enforcement Mechanism:**
- Year 1 (D rating): No immediate consequences, monitoring required
- Year 2 (D rating): Increased scrutiny from flag state, charterer concerns
- Year 3 (D rating): **MANDATORY** corrective action plan submission
- Year 4: Must achieve C rating or face sanctions (port state control detention, charter market exclusion)
