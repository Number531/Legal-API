## Appendix B: Verification Tag Methodology

### Tag Type Definitions

**[VERIFIED:source]**
- **Purpose:** Documents direct database/website source confirming citation accuracy
- **Examples:**
  - `[VERIFIED: search_us_code]` - U.S. Code retrieved via LII/Cornell or GPO
  - `[VERIFIED: ecfr.gov]` - C.F.R. retrieved from eCFR.gov
  - `[VERIFIED: NRC ADAMS]` - NRC document retrieved from ADAMS database
  - `[VERIFIED: CourtListener]` - Case law retrieved from CourtListener database
- **Quality Standard:** Direct retrieval from authoritative government database or authenticated legal research platform

**[INFERRED:precedent]**
- **Purpose:** Documents analogical legal reasoning from related precedent
- **Examples:**
  - `[INFERRED: analogical reasoning from EDF-Calvert Cliffs 49.99% French ownership precedent to QIA 20% Qatari ownership]`
  - `[INFERRED: extension of Duke Power Co. antitrust standing to nuclear M&A context]`
- **Quality Standard:** Logical extension of established legal principles to factually analogous situations with documented limiting principles

**[METHODOLOGY:analysis]**
- **Purpose:** Documents quantitative/probabilistic analysis methodology
- **Examples:**
  - `[METHODOLOGY: Monte Carlo simulation, 1000 iterations, correlation matrix rho=0.65]`
  - `[METHODOLOGY: Expected value calculation: 85% liability probability x $1.125M exposure]`
  - `[METHODOLOGY: DCF analysis with 8.5% WACC, 15-year projection period]`
- **Quality Standard:** Reproducible quantitative technique with documented assumptions and parameters

**[ASSUMED:limitation]**
- **Purpose:** Documents data limitations and analytical assumptions
- **Examples:**
  - `[ASSUMED: NIEC PPA full contract not available in data room; analysis based on summary term sheet]`
  - `[ASSUMED: APH financial statements not provided; analysis assumes Blackstone sponsor support]`
  - `[ASSUMED: CBA negotiation history dates estimated from typical 2-year bargaining cycle]`
- **Quality Standard:** Explicit acknowledgment of missing data with reasoned assumption documented

### Tag Application Standards

**Statutory Citations (U.S.C.):**
- **Minimum Required:** [VERIFIED:source]
- **Example:** "42 U.S.C. § 2184 [VERIFIED: search_us_code]"

**Regulatory Citations (C.F.R.):**
- **Minimum Required:** [VERIFIED:source]
- **Example:** "10 C.F.R. § 50.80 [VERIFIED: ecfr.gov]"

**Case Law Citations:**
- **Minimum Required:** [VERIFIED:source] OR [INFERRED:precedent]
- **Example:** "Duke Power Co. v. Carolina Envtl. Study Grp., 438 U.S. 59 (1978) [VERIFIED: CourtListener]"
- **Example:** "Analogous to Calvert Cliffs precedent [INFERRED: 49.99% French ownership approval precedent]"

**Quantitative Findings:**
- **Minimum Required:** [METHODOLOGY:analysis] + [VERIFIED:source] for underlying data
- **Example:** "Expected value $5.91M [METHODOLOGY: 85% liability x $6.95M exposure] [VERIFIED: employment-labor-report.md lines 450-475]"

**Data Limitations:**
- **Required When:** Missing data requires analytical assumption
- **Example:** "NIEC PPA expires 2035 [ASSUMED: contract summary indicates 15-year term from 2020 signing; full contract not in data room]"
