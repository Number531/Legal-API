## BLUEBOOK RULE 10.9 COMPLIANCE ANALYSIS

### Rule Statement
**Bluebook Rule 10.9 (Short Forms for Cases)**:
- **First citation**: Must be full Bluebook citation with complete party names, reporter, and year
- **Subsequent citations in same general discussion**: May use short form (*Case Name*, volume reporter at page)
- **Id.**: May be used when citing immediately preceding authority

### Application to This Document

**Document Structure**:
1. Executive Summary (Sections I-III) - Lines 177-685
2. Questions Presented (Section II) - Lines 470-522
3. Brief Answers (Section III) - Lines 522-685
4. Detailed Analysis (Section IV.A-IV.F) - Lines 685-7980

**Issue**: The document treats Executive Summary as separate from Detailed Analysis, but Bluebook Rule 10.9 requires full citation on **first reference in document**, not first reference in each analytical section.
