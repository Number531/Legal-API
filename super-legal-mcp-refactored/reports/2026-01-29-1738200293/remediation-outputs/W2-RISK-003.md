**Discount Rate**: All NPV calculations use 8% discount rate reflecting estimated weighted average cost of capital (WACC) for corporate acquirers in distressed manufacturing sector. Rate should be adjusted for acquirer-specific hurdle rates (strategic buyers: 8-12%; financial buyers: 15-20%; foreign buyers: 10-14%).

**Probability Basis**: Probabilities derived from:
- EPA ECHO database violation prevalence (60-90% manufacturing sector)
- Documented Western Pennsylvania manufacturing bankruptcy precedent (LTV Steel, Wheeling-Pittsburgh, Bethlehem Steel, Allegheny Ludlum, U.S. Steel Clairton, Westinghouse Sharon)
- Federal court enforcement records and consent decrees
- Third Circuit bankruptcy court environmental decisions (*PA DER v. Conroy*, *Torwico Electronics*)
- Supreme Court precedent (*Midlantic*, *Ohio v. Kovacs*)

**Exposure Calculation**: NPV methodology applies discounted cash flow (DCF) for near-term costs with defined timeline (investigation, interim measures, final remedy construction in Years 1-7) plus net present value (NPV) for long-term monitoring obligations extending 30+ years (Years 8-38). Calculation: Σ [Cost_t / (1+0.08)^t] where t = years 1-38.

**Facility Size Categories**: Based on documented Western PA manufacturing facility patterns:
- Small (<5 acres): Metal fabrication, machine shops, specialty manufacturing with limited process operations
- Medium (5-50 acres): Integrated manufacturing facilities, steel service centers, chemical formulation plants with multiple process units
- Large (>50 acres): Integrated steel mills, coke plants, primary metal production facilities with extensive infrastructure

**Cost Ranges - Low/High Scenarios**:
