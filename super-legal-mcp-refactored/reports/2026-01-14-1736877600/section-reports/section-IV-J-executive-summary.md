#### Escrow Mechanics

**Escrow Agent:** Bank of New York Mellon (or comparable institutional trustee)

**Indemnification Claims Against Escrow:**
- **Threshold (Basket):** $2.5M aggregate (0.07% of transaction value) - only claims exceeding threshold draw on escrow
- **Individual Claim Minimum:** $100K (tipping basket; amounts below threshold count toward basket but aren't individually recoverable)
- **Escrow Cap:** $400M (aggregate maximum recovery from escrow)
- **No Double Recovery:** Claims paid from escrow reduce available R&W insurance limit

**Claim Resolution Process:**
1. Buyer provides written notice to Seller and Escrow Agent describing indemnification claim, amount, and basis
2. Seller has 30 days to dispute claim (if no dispute, Escrow Agent releases funds to Buyer)
3. If disputed, parties negotiate 60 days; if unresolved, binding arbitration (AAA Commercial Rules, 1 arbitrator, Delaware venue)
4. Arbitration decision binds Escrow Agent within 10 days

**Tax Treatment of Escrow:**
- Seller treats escrow as "open transaction" (no tax on escrowed amount until released)
- If amounts paid to Buyer as indemnification, Seller recognizes ordinary income (reduction in sale proceeds)
- If amounts released to Seller, Seller recognizes capital gain (as part of original sale proceeds)
