### Liability Type Classification Guide

| Type | Characteristics | Correct Methodology | Formula |
|------|-----------------|---------------------|---------|
| **Perpetual** | Recurring annually, no end date | NPV | Annual Amount ÷ Discount Rate |
| **Contingent** | Single event, uncertain outcome | EV (Expected Value) | Probability × Magnitude |
| **Hybrid** | Multi-year program, defined timeline | DCF | Σ (CF_t ÷ (1+r)^t) |

### Classification Examples

- BLET labor settlement $78M-$105M/year recurring
- PTC annual operating costs $120M/year regulatory requirement
- NPV assumes 8% WACC: $78M ÷ 8% = $975M | $105M ÷ 8% = $1,312.5M | $120M ÷ 8% = $1,500M
