## OBJECTIVITY CRITERIA

| Check | Criteria | Standard |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Adverse Authority** | Acknowledges precedents unfavorable to acquirer position | Required |
| **Counter-Arguments** | Each material finding includes target's likely counter-position | Required |
| **Advocacy Language** | Free from "clearly," "obviously," "must," "undoubtedly" | Required |
| **Uncertainty Acknowledged** | Flags genuine legal uncertainty (circuit splits, unsettled law) | Required |
| **Balanced Probabilities** | Estimates distributed (not all >80% or all <20%) | Recommended |
