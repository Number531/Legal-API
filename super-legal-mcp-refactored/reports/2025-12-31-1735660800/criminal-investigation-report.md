### F. Deferred Prosecution Agreement (DPA) Likelihood

#### 1. DPA vs. Indictment â€” DOJ Decision Factors

**What is a Deferred Prosecution Agreement?**

A DPA is an agreement between DOJ and a defendant whereby:
- Defendant admits to criminal conduct
- DOJ files criminal charges but AGREES NOT TO PROSECUTE
- Defendant pays criminal fine + implements compliance reforms + cooperates with government
- If defendant complies with DPA terms for specified period (typically 2-5 years), charges are dismissed
- If defendant violates DPA, DOJ prosecutes using admitted factsÂ²â¶

**DOJ Factors for DPA vs. Indictment (U.S. Attorneys' Manual Â§ 9-28.000):**
1. Nature and seriousness of offense
2. Pervasiveness of wrongdoing within corporation
3. Corporation's history of similar conduct
4. Corporation's willingness to cooperate
5. Existence and effectiveness of compliance program
6. Remedial actions taken by corporation
7. Collateral consequences (impact on innocent shareholders, employees, customers)

**Cryptocurrency Exchange DPA Precedents:**
