**Matter:** $1.8 Billion Acquisition of Pinnacle Investment Management, Inc. by Global Asset Partners LLC

**Prepared for:** Global Asset Partners LLC Board of Directors

**Date:** January 23, 2026

**Transaction Overview:** Global Asset Partners LLC ("GAP"), a New York-based private equity firm managing $8.5 billion in committed capital through Global Asset Partners Fund IV, proposes to acquire 100% of the outstanding stock of Pinnacle Investment Management, Inc. ("Pinnacle"), a Delaware corporation registered with the SEC as an investment adviser (File No. 801-45678) managing $42.5 billion in assets across institutional separate accounts ($23.4 billion), registered mutual funds ($12.8 billion), and two hedge funds ($6.3 billion).
