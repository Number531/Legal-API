#### 2. Seattle Terminal Likely Not Subject to TWIC Reader Mandate (60-70% Probability)

**Risk Group A Classification Analysis:**

33 CFR Â§ 105.253 designates facilities as "Risk Group A" (mandatory biometric TWIC readers) or non-Risk Group A (visual or electronic TWIC inspection at operator discretion) based on operational profile. [VERIFIED: 33 CFR Â§ 105.253]

**Risk Group A Criteria:**

1. **Passenger Vessel Facilities** (effective June 8, 2020): Receive vessels certificated to carry >1,000 passengers
2. **CDC Facilities â€” Category 1** (effective May 8, 2026): Handle Certain Dangerous Cargoes in **bulk** AND **transfer** such cargoes from or to a vessel
3. **CDC Facilities â€” Category 2** (effective May 8, 2026): Handle CDC in **bulk** but do NOT transfer it from or to a vessel
4. **CDC Facilities â€” Category 3** (effective May 8, 2029): Receive vessels carrying CDC in bulk but do NOT transfer during vessel-to-facility interface

**"Certain Dangerous Cargoes" Definition (33 CFR Â§ 160.202):**

- Division 1.1, 1.2, or 1.3 explosives
- Division 2.1 flammable gases **in bulk**
- Division 2.3 poisonous gases **in bulk**
- Class 3 flammable liquids **in bulk**
- Division 5.1 oxidizers **in bulk**
- Class 6.1 toxic materials **in bulk**

[VERIFIED: 33 CFR Â§ 160.202]

**Critical Distinction: "In Bulk" vs. "Containerized":**

- **"In bulk"** = Liquid or gaseous cargo carried directly in ship tanks/holds without packaging (tankers, bulk carriers)
