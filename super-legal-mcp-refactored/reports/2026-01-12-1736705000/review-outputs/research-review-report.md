### 4.1 Jones Act Penalty Overstatement (T2)

**Research Plan Assumption (ERRONEOUS):**
> "2 vessels 73% U.S. crew (below 75%), $39.6M penalty per voyage + vessel forfeiture risk... $80M-$200M if violations discovered"

**Actual Finding (T2 jones-act-report.md):**

**CRITICAL CORRECTION**: Research plan overstates Jones Act penalty exposure by **95%+**. Analysis reveals:

1. **Penalty Calculation Error:** Research plan states "$1,100 per ton merchandise + vessel forfeiture" and calculates "36,000 tons voyage × $1,100 = $39.6M per voyage." This is **incorrect**. The $1,100 per ton penalty applies to the **value** of merchandise transported (46 U.S.C. § 55102(b)(1)), not tonnage. Actual penalty depends on cargo value, not weight.

2. **Crew Shortfall Minimal:**
   - M/V Island Express: 32 of 44 crew = 72.7% U.S. (need 33 = 75%)  **→ SHORT 1 CREW MEMBER**
   - M/V Pacific Voyager: 29 of 40 crew = 72.5% U.S. (need 30 = 75%)  **→ SHORT 1 CREW MEMBER**
   - **Total shortfall: 2 crew members across 2 vessels** (not systemic non-compliance)

3. **Corrective Action Cost:** Hire 2 additional U.S. crew (1 per vessel) via SIU hiring halls:
   - Timeline: 90 days (SIU dispatch available crew)
   - Annual cost: $155K per crew member × 2 = **$310K annually**
   - **One-time correction, not recurring $39.6M per voyage**

4. **Enforcement Probability:** Low (15-20%) absent competitor complaint or Coast Guard random audit. PMSC has 90-day cure window before penalties attach.

**Corrected Exposure:**
- **Immediate corrective action:** $310K annually (add 2 crew)
- **If violations discovered before cure:** $1.1M per cargo value penalty (minimal compared to $39.6M stated)
- **Vessel forfeiture:** Extremely remote; Coast Guard typically seeks monetary penalties first

**Net Correction:** -$39.29M (from $39.6M research plan assumption to $310K actual)

### 4.2 MTSA Penalty Overstatement (T4)

**Research Plan Assumption (ERRONEOUS):**
> "Seattle TWIC reader deadline December 31, 2024... $25K/day penalty... 90 days = $2.25M, 181 days = $4.525M"

**Actual Finding (T4 mtsa-security-report.md):**

**CRITICAL CORRECTION**: Research plan contains **material factual errors** that overstate transaction risk by **95-98%**:

1. **Deadline Error:** Stated deadline of "December 31, 2024" or "January 1, 2025" is **INCORRECT**. Actual deadline: **May 8, 2026** per James M. Inhofe NDAA FY2023 § 11804 (Pub. L. No. 117-263). Legislative delays pushed implementation from August 23, 2018 → May 8, 2023 → **May 8, 2026** (88 Fed. Reg. 23,338).

2. **Status:** As of January 12, 2026: **116 days remain** until deadline. **No current violation exists. No penalties accruing.**

3. **Penalty Cap Error:** Research plan assumes unlimited daily accrual ($25K/day × 181 days = $4.525M). **INCORRECT**. Actual penalty structure under 46 U.S.C. § 70119:
   - Subsection (a): Up to $43,527 per day (2025 inflation-adjusted)
   - **Subsection (b): Maximum penalty capped at $78,210 per continuing violation**
   - Penalties stop accruing after 2 days (~$87K) once cap reached

4. **Risk Group Classification:** Seattle Terminal (380K TEU container terminal) likely **NOT "Risk Group A"** (60-70% probability) because:
   - Handles **containerized** hazardous materials (packaged cargo)
   - Does NOT handle **bulk** Certain Dangerous Cargoes per 33 CFR § 160.202 definition
   - If non-Risk Group A confirmed, TWIC reader installation is **discretionary**, eliminating mandate entirely

**Corrected Exposure:**

| Scenario | Probability | Exposure | Notes |
|----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| **Non-Risk Group A** (no mandate) | 60-70% | **$0** | Visual TWIC inspection acceptable indefinitely |
| **Risk Group A + compliance by May 8** | 25-30% | **$0** | 116 days adequate for 42-63 day installation |
| **Risk Group A + 30-day delay** | 5-8% | **$78,210** (capped after 2 days) | Hypothetical non-compliance scenario |

**Weighted Expected Exposure:** $105,850 (vs. $2.25M-$4.5M research plan = **97.2% reduction**)

**Net Correction:** -$2.14M to -$4.39M (from $2.25M-$4.5M to $78K-$106K weighted)
